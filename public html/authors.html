Who Are The Creators Of This Fan Page?

Blake Kakacek


Blake Kakacek has been an avid Star Wars fan for a long time. Blake aspires to find his true calling and incorporate his hobbies in his future career. He is currently a sophomore at Simpson College and planning on a career in Bio-informatics, which involves a blend of Biology, Computer Science, and Computer Informational Systems. While that may not involve Star Wars, he works with the Star Wars Club by writing stories and editing the website.

JJ Kosobucki

JJ Kosobucki shares a profound passion for Star Wars with the co-author of the fan page: Blake Kakacek. JJ currently attends Simpson College and is passionate about technology. He plans to major in Computer Informational Systems and minor in Computer Science. He is a very talented programmer, and he anxiously awaits his chance to apply his skills in a professional setting in the real world.

Sam Adeniyi

Sam Adeniyi is a Co - Author of this page. He has limited knowledge of Star Wars, however has a love for technology as he is majoring in Computer Science and Computer Information Systems.
