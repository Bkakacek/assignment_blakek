What is the First Order?

Initially, the First Order was comprised of the remnants of the Galactic Empire in the Unknown Regions
of the galaxy. After the fall of the Galactic Empire, the remaining members of the Empire waited around three years
to assemble a strong enough force to pose a threat. The First Order aimed to reinstate the ideals of the Galactic Empire,
but the increased number of members allowed the splinter group to become its own faction.

First Order Dilemma on Prora 9

The First Order is in pursuit of the Resistance forces who have charted a course to a jungle planet
known as Prora 9. A victory over the Resistance could provide a significant advantage over the enemy. Acquiring the planet
of Prora 9 could provide the First Order with a new factory to produce updated weaponry, and another planet would expand
the First Order's empire.

**Side Note**

Many people have noted the similarity between Nazi Germany and the First Order in the film The Force Awakens. The author claims that First Order is inspired by the theory of ODESSA.

Win Conditions:

Wipe out the pirates. Defeat the Resistance. Control the factory.

The First Order has the capacity to decimate the Resistance on Prora 9.
